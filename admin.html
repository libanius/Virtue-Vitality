How it Works

1. After completing their order, customers are asked if they'd like to Pay it Forward by leaving a credit.

2. The credit is added to the blessing bank (shared storage, visible to all).

3. The next customer to reach checkout will see a message that a stranger has paid for their order.

4. One credit is used per checkout. If the credit runs out, normal checkout continues.

5. The recipient is then also invited to Pay it Forward — creating a chain of generosity.
