How it Works

1. After a successful purchase, customers are invited to leave a credit in the blessing bank.

2. Credits are stored in persistent shared storage — they survive page reloads and are visible to all sessions.

3. The next customer to reach the payment screen finds a message: a stranger has paid for their order.

4. One credit is consumed per checkout event. If no credits remain, normal checkout continues.

5. The recipient is then invited to pay it forward — creating a chain of generosity.
